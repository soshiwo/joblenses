Technical Footnote: Reliability Index

The Profile Reliability metric is determined through a statistical variance analysis (population variance) of the candidate's scores across the 10 scenarios. A 'High Profile Stability' indicates that the candidate demonstrates consistent prioritization of their core values regardless of the situational context or competing pressures. 'Low Profile Stability' flags high variance, suggesting the candidate's behavioral choices are highly context-dependent, fluid, or potentially contradictory when balancing corporate governance against business outcomes.
